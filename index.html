About Me

My name is Alden, and I am in 10th grade. I'm on the JV tennis team for my school, and we did really well last season! Tennis is also one of my favorite hobbies, along with making matcha at home, thrifting, and especially going to the beach. I spend my summers on the East Coast with my family, and that is why summer is my favorite season! It depends on the day, but I love to experiment with different types of coffee and matcha - my favorite is matcha with vanilla syrup. My family and I love cooking and baking, so we always try to find new recipes to make! Also, a fun fact about me is that I have 2 dogs named Teddy and Scout!
