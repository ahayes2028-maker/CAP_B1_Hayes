Best Work

Throughout this year in Intro to Comp Sci, I ultimately noticed that my best project was the Unit 3 Create Your Own Game. I had a lot of fun creating the game, and I'm excited to share it with you! In this game, the player's goal is to collect the required number of coins (200) without losing health from the fireballs. At the beginning of the game, the player starts with the score set to 0 and the health set to 100. Each time the player collects a coin, the score increases by 5. If the player collides with a fireball, the health decreases by 10.

All you need to do to play the game is move the witch by pressing the up, down, right, and left arrow keys. It is important that you don't let the witch out of the frame/border!

To win the game, the player needs to reach a score of 200, which means they've collected 40 coins! Now, to lose the game, the player's health needs to reach 0. And yes, it is possible! Good luck :)
