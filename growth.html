Growth

Even though this was one of my first projects in this class, I think this really showed my growth from not knowing any computer science to being able to create an app! This is my App Lab Code Along project, and this is definitely one of my favorite projects I've created. I wanted people to understand their hair type, how they could take care of it, and even learn new styles so they felt more confident! Even though it took a long time to make, I think this app could be successful in the real world. Some of my favorite things that the app includes are interactive buttons, a personalized hair quiz, in-app video tutorials, and especially the chat where you can ask your questions to a professional! You can access all of the different pages on the menu bar at the bottom! I hope you enjoy this app, and I hope it helps you!
